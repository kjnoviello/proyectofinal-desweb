GRAND SCAURIZA HOTEL

Prepare to be delighted when you stay at Panama’s first all inclusive hotel & resort Grand Scauriza. Steeped in history and full of charm, Grand Scauriza Hotel is located right in the heart of historic Panama. We offer an oasis of calm within easy walking distance of many of Panama’s most memorable sights. Let us share our manifesto with you and the story of how we turned this historic colonial home into a beautiful hotel.

Welcome to the Grand Scauriza Hotel, a luxurious and modern hotel located in the heart of Panama. Our hotel is the perfect choice for both business and leisure travelers, with a wide range of amenities and services designed to make your stay comfortable and enjoyable. At the Grand Scauriza Hotel, we pride ourselves on our world-class amenities. Take a dip in our rooftop pool, which offers stunning views of the city, or work up a sweat in our state-of-the-art fitness center. Relax and unwind at our spa, which offers a variety of treatments and services designed to leave you feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

Our hotel also features a variety of dining options, including our rooftop bar and restaurant, which serves delicious cocktails and local cuisine. For business travelers, we offer a range of meeting and event spaces, as well as a business center with all of the amenities you need to stay productive on the road.

Our rooms are spacious and well-appointed, with comfortable beds, plush linens, and modern amenities such as mini-fridges, coffee makers, and flat-screen TVs. We offer a variety of room types to suit your needs, from standard rooms to executive suites. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always on hand to help you with anything you need during your stay, from arranging transportation to recommending local attractions and restaurants. Come experience the luxury and comfort of the Grand Scauriza Hotel for yourself. We look forward to welcoming you soon !
