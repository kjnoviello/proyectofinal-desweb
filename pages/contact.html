The Grand Scauriza Hotel is located in Panama, a beautiful country in Central America that boasts stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and vibrant nightlife. The hotel is situated in a prime location that offers easy access to some of the city's most popular attractions, as well as a wide variety of restaurants and entertainment venues. One of the closest attractions to the hotel is the famous Panama Canal, a marvel of engineering that is a must-see for any visitor to Panama. The Panama Canal is just a short drive away from the hotel and offers a fascinating glimpse into the history and culture of this incredible country. Another popular attraction near the hotel is the Casco Viejo, a historic district that is home to some of Panama's most iconic landmarks and cultural institutions. The Casco Viejo is a UNESCO World Heritage site and offers a unique blend of old-world charm and modern sophistication.

Finally, the nearest airport to the Grand Scauriza Hotel is Tocumen International Airport, which is located approximately 20 kilometers away. The airport is easily accessible via taxi or public transportation, and guests of the hotel can arrange for airport transportation through the hotel's concierge service.
