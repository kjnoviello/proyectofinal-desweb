Available services and commodities


The Grand Scauriza Hotel is a premier destination for travelers seeking luxury and comfort in the heart of Panama. From the moment you step through the doors, you'll be greeted by a team of friendly and professional staff members who are dedicated to providing exceptional service and making your stay as comfortable as possible.


One of the hallmarks of the Grand Scauriza Hotel is its commitment to providing guests with a range of services and amenities that cater to their every need. Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, you'll find that the hotel offers everything you need to make your stay a success. For business travelers, the Grand Scauriza Hotel offers a range of amenities and services that are designed to help them stay connected and productive. This includes high-speed internet access, a fully equipped business center, and meeting and conference rooms that are perfect for hosting events and presentations.

If you're looking to relax and unwind during your stay, the hotel has plenty of options to choose from. The on-site spa and wellness center offers a range of treatments and services that are designed to help you rejuvenate your body and mind, while the fitness center is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment that will help you stay in shape.
